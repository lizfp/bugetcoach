About Me

Hi there! I am someone who thrives on connecting with others and helping them find the tools and confidence they need to succeed. With a rich background in law, marketing, and teaching, I’ve always been passionate about empowering people. As a budget coach, my goal is to make financial education accessible and relatable, whether you're an individual looking to improve your personal finances or a small business owner wanting a clearer financial picture. I'm here to guide you with practical advice and personalized support. Let's work together to make your financial goals a reality!

How it Works

I offer easy-to-follow sessions to help you take control of your finances. Whether you're looking to save more, earn extra income, or spend more mindfully, I’ll guide you through it in a way that's clear and practical. You can choose from group sessions, where we tackle common financial challenges together, or one-on-one coaching, where I’ll create a personalized plan just for you.

How I Can Help You

  1. Find Ways to Save: Together, we’ll identify areas where you can cut back on spending and save more.
  2. Increase Your Income: Explore extra income opportunities like side jobs or freelancing.
  3. Spend More Mindfully: Align spending with your values and goals to make better daily decisions.
  4. Simplify Your Finances: Learn easy strategies to budget and track expenses without stress.
  5. Personalized Support: Group or one-on-one sessions tailored to your financial needs and goals.

Ready to take control of your finances? Let’s work together to make money management easy, empowering, and stress-free.

Services

Group Sessions for Organizations

I offer group sessions tailored to help organizations improve their employees’ financial literacy. These sessions cover topics like saving strategies, increasing income, and mindful spending, all in a simple and accessible way.

One-on-One Coaching

For personalized guidance, I offer individual coaching sessions where we work together to create a custom financial plan. Whether it’s budgeting, saving, or managing your money more effectively, these sessions are tailored to your specific needs and goals.
